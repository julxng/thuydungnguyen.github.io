Would You Understand

Question: How can we create intimate connections through shared physiological rhythms?

A two-person prototype that captures heartbeats via smartphone camera (rPPG) and transmits them as haptic vibrations through Bluetooth-connected wearable panels. Built with Arduino, vibrotactile actuators, and custom soft-skin housing.

Sonic Exploration

Question: How can we rediscover mindful presence through the sounds of everyday life?

An interactive sound installation featuring 20 speakers, Arduino hardware, and MaxMSP. Visitors identify sounds from nature, humanity, and surroundings in a playful sonic puzzle that challenges perception and memory.

About

I'm a studio artist who codes, working at the intersection of haptic interfaces, biosensing, and interactive sound. My practice explores how technology can create new forms of intimacy, mindfulness, and embodied experience through physiological data, tactile feedback, and spatial audio.

I work primarily with Arduino hardware, vibrotactile actuators, smartphone sensing (rPPG), MaxMSP, and multi-channel audio systems. My approach emphasizes rapid prototyping, user-centered design, and exploring the emotional and perceptual dimensions of technology. Currently developing projects for applications to creative technology graduate programs including MIT Media Lab.

Education: BA Studio Arts, Bard College (2023)
