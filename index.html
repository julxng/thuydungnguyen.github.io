About

I'm a studio artist who codes, working at the intersection of haptic interfaces, biosensing, and interactive sound. My practice explores how technology can create new forms of intimacy, mindfulness, and embodied experience.

I work primarily with Arduino hardware, vibrotactile actuators, smartphone sensing (rPPG), MaxMSP, and multi-channel audio systems. Currently developing projects for applications to creative technology graduate programs including MIT Media Lab.

Education: BA Studio Arts, Bard College (2023)
